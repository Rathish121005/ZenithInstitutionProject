Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2025

At ZenithBRAIN, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information when you fill out our admission form:

  • Student’s Full Name
  • Class & Course Preferences
  • Parent/Guardian Names
  • School Name
  • Residential Address
  • Phone Number
  • Email Address (optional)
2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Process admission applications
  • Communicate updates and inquiries
  • Maintain secure internal records
  • Improve our educational services
3. Legal Basis for Processing

We collect and process your data based on your consent. You have the right to withdraw your consent at any time.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration.

5. Third-Party Sharing

Your information is never sold or rented. We may share it only when:

  • Required by law or authorities
  • Necessary to protect student safety
  • You provide explicit consent
6. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent. If found, such data will be promptly deleted.

7. Your Rights
  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent at any time
8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
